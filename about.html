About Us


The We for She Campaign aims to create a safe space that fosters an inclusive environment for dialogue, action, and accountability to acknowledge that feminism empowers all people.
We were inspired by Emma Watson's speech at the United Nation's on September 20th, 2014. In her speech, Emma Watson invited all men to join in the fight for feminism. While we support Emma Watson's message, we wanted to create an invitation for all, regardless of gender identity, race, religion, ethnicity, or sexual orientation. WE are for the benefit of all.
